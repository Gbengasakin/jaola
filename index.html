About JAOLA

JAOLA was founded with a clear mission - to bridge gaps in social services through justice-centered approaches that empower communities.

Our team combines expertise in healthcare, legal advocacy, and community organizing to deliver holistic support systems that recognize the dignity of every individual.

Since 2020, we've helped over 500 families access critical services while advocating for systemic change that addresses root causes of inequality.
